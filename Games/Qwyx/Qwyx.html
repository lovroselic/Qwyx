About:

QWYX is a clone of Qix, originally released in 1981 by developers Randy and Sandy Pfeiffer. Although its appearance might seem rudimentary - reminiscent of a glorified screensaver - it was considered one of the most innovative games in the early 1980s.

This clone aims to stay true to the original, with only minor modifications.

My own algorithm for identifying the starting point for the Flood Fill operation is detailed on GameDev Stack Exchange. It is notably swift and efficient.
